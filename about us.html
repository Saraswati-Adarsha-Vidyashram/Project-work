About Us

Saraswati Adarsha Vidyashram was established on 13th Magh 2049, Basanta Panchami with 9 staffs and 160 students. The school is run by a team of eminent academicians and prominent professionals under the leadership of Mr. Ganesh Man Singh Bhandari and Dr. Radha Bhandari, well-experienced academicians who had contributed their time in the teaching sector with the maxim: "GO FORWARD", to meet the need of individual and nation as a whole through wide range of knowledge, skill, modern methods, equipment, etc. to provide highly competent students. The principal objective of SAV is focused for the children of its locality to provide quality education with the hope to enhance development in the society. The school has about 1000 students and 60 faculty members running Montessori to class 10 at present.

The school is spread over 10 ropanies (55 thousand square feet) of land with awesome natural beauty.The school has its own four storeys building. The school believes in effective and systematic teaching management and it has become the key factor for the growing attraction to increasing number of students every year. The school believes in practical methods of teaching. The school is implementing Montessori method of teaching to young children to make it a real and joyful learning.
